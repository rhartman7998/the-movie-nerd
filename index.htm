Halloween is a film by John Carter made in 1978. This was the kick-off film that spawned a franchise including 12 sequels. The film series was orginally intended to be an anthology; which is why Michale Meyers did not apear in the third film. Halloween 1978 follows a mental patient who escapes from the hospital to go back to his hometown and kill the last remaining member of his family.
